in order of priority
chkblood(check blood, when turn is passed, reset to 0)
saccard(sacrifice card 1-4 on table)
chkhand(check cards in hand)
chktable(prints table contents as table heh)
turn(end turn, apply combat)
chkscale(checks the value of the scale, print for readability as towards each direction rather than just an int from -5 to 5?)
chkcard(check specific one in hand for cost and power, or on table: T(able)E(enemy)1-4 TP(layer)1-4 H(and)1-max)

store player cards in three lists, hand deck, active deck, inactive deck

active deck is the deck during combat, when the play takes a card it is removed from the active deck and moved to the hand deck
